MY HERO ACADEMIA

My Hero Academia manga is written and drawn by Kōhei Horikoshi. It has been pre-published since July 7, 2014 in Shūeisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine. The story is based on a short story made seven years earlier by the author.

Izuku Midoriya aka "Deku" has 7 Quirks in My Hero Academia. These Quirks are previous bearers of One for All and are manifestations of it.

Shoto Todoroki, Ice and Fire Quirk. This Quirk is the subject of a Quirk marriage: The flames come from Shoto's father, Enji Todoroki, and the ice comes from his mother, Rei Todoroki.

Katsuki Bakugo, his Quirk is Explosion, which allows him to make explosions from his hands by detonating the nitroglycerin-like substance he sweats.

Tenya Lida, Tenya's main power is "Engine," which is his Quirk. It takes the form of engine-like tubes on his calves, giving him incredible speed and kicking strength.

My Hero Academia’s sixth season teases a darker turn of events for Deku and his friends as heroes from U.A. High School fight tooth and nail against the Paranormal Liberation Front. On top of everything, Shigaraki’s has unlocked a whole new level of his Decay quirk since the season 5 finale.
